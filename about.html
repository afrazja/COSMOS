The human behind it

Why COSMOS exists

Hi — I'm Afraz.

I'm an entrepreneur, and for as long as I can remember I've been fascinated by outer space and by one question in particular: is there life beyond our planet? I've watched more documentaries than I can count and read stacks of novels about humans pushing out into the dark. Somewhere along the way, that fascination turned into two convictions.

First: we have barely started. For all our rockets, rovers and telescopes, humanity still needs to discover far, far more before we can truly reach the distances we dream about. The map is mostly blank. That's not discouraging — it means the biggest chapters haven't been written yet, and the people who will write them are alive right now.

Second: the way we teach space is weak — and the myths are strong. Most people meet the universe through headlines and movie physics. Real space education, the kind that makes a young person say "I could work on that", is rare. I believe that gap is costing us explorers.

COSMOS is my contribution to closing it: a place where the universe is shown honestly and beautifully — real NASA and ESA imagery, real numbers, and where we use AI-generated art, we say so — built especially for young people who feel the pull but haven't been told the path exists. That's why the site doesn't end with a pretty picture; it ends with a page about finding your own place in space.

And this is only step one. What I really want to build is a platform — a place where curious people share their thinking, challenge the myths, contribute ideas toward the discoveries ahead, and give each other the motivation to attempt big things. A crew, not an audience. If that excites you, I'd genuinely like to hear from you.

— Afraz

The three rules

What COSMOS stands for

🔭 Honest first

Real photography and real numbers, checked against NASA, ESA and agency sources. Where we use AI-generated concept art, it's labeled — always. Wonder doesn't need exaggeration.

🌱 Aimed at the young

Every page tries to leave a young visitor with the same feeling: this is real, it's still beginning, and there's a seat with your name on it. Careers, subjects, first steps — not just pretty pictures.

🚀 Built together

The future of this project is a community platform: sharing ideas, breaking myths, and motivating each other toward the discoveries ahead. It starts with an email — yours.

Full transparency

How this site was made

COSMOS was built in a matter of days as a collaboration between me and Claude, an AI assistant by Anthropic — I set the vision, direction and taste (and generated the concept artwork with AI image tools); Claude wrote the code and helped shape the words. I'm sharing that openly because it's part of the point: the tools for building ambitious things have never been this accessible. If a non-native English speaker with a laptop can ship this in days, imagine what you could build.
